About Me

I'm Nikola Nakovski, a designer and technologist passionate about building digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional. My work spans UI/UX design, web development, and writing about the evolving relationship between technology and human discipline.

I believe that discipline is the foundation of creativity. In a world increasingly shaped by AI, maintaining a disciplined approach to learning and creating is more important than ever. My portfolio showcases projects and thoughts at this intersection.
